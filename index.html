The world had forgotten terror. Just a mere illusion of the heavens. Flowers Bloomimg, children smiling and harmony in the air. Human beings were among the earthy beings enjoying such a paradise and thriving gracefully in it. Unfortunately, Humanity did not know how to do away with its insticts of selfishness, greed and lust. Though humanity had its flaws, the world was still one of bliss. It all changed when humanity committed the most foul transgression. Humans had forgotten god!

Since then, us of the underworld took over. We have summoned Beasts of the underworld to reek terror and chaos. Humanity deserves to sufffer. Each day we live in delights witnessing the souls that we have tormented. What once was a perfect world is now a slaughter house for us. We have turned seas into blood pools and created mountains of corpses. They deserved it.

In this new haven of pain and tears was hope for humanity. Human warriors had emerged to fight us off. But do they really stand a chance?
